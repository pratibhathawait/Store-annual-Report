📊 Store Annual Report Analysis
📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing a store’s annual sales performance to derive actionable insights related to revenue, customer behavior, and business growth.
Annual reports are essential for understanding overall business performance, including sales trends, customer segments, and operational efficiency over a specific period.

🎯 Objectives
    Analyze yearly sales and order performance
    Identify key customer segments (age, gender, location)
    Evaluate sales contribution across different channels
    Understand product category performance
    Provide insights to improve business strategy and revenue
🛠️ Tools & Technologies
    Excel (Data Cleaning, Pivot Tables, Dashboard Creation)
    Data Visualization (Charts, KPIs, Slicers)
    Analytics Techniques (Exploratory Data Analysis - EDA)
📂 Dataset
The dataset includes:
    Customer demographics (Age, Gender, State)
    Order and sales data
    Product categories
    Sales channels (Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra)
    Order status and delivery details
🔍 Process
    Verified dataset for missing values and inconsistencies
    Cleaned and formatted data (handled null values, corrected data types)
    Created pivot tables to analyze key KPIs
    Developed an interactive dashboard for visualization
    Used slicers to enable dynamic filtering and insights
📊 Key Analysis Performed
    Sales vs Orders comparison
    Monthly sales and order trends
    Gender-based purchase analysis
    Age group vs order distribution
    Channel-wise sales contribution
    Top-performing states
    Order status distribution
    Highest selling product category
📈 Key Insights
    👩 Women customers contributed ~65% of total sales
    👥 Age group 30–49 years contributed ~50% of orders
    📍 Top states: Maharashtra, Karnataka, Uttar Pradesh
    🛒 Major channels: Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra
    📦 Over 90% of orders were successfully delivered
    📊 Identified highest-selling categories and peak sales months
    📊 Dashboard

The dashboard provides:
    Sales vs Orders trend visualization
    Customer segmentation (Age & Gender)
    State-wise and channel-wise performance
    Order status and category insights
💡 Business Recommendations
    Focus marketing efforts on women aged 30–49
    Target high-performing states for better ROI
    Strengthen presence on top sales channels
    Offer personalized promotions and discounts
    Improve inventory planning for high-demand categories
🚀 Conclusion
This project demonstrates how annual sales data can be transformed into meaningful business insights. By focusing on high-value customer segments and optimizing marketing strategies, businesses can significantly enhance sales performance and customer engagement.
